about this space
This is a quiet place to imagine a conversation with Jesus. You type; a voice imagined by an AI responds in the way Jesus might, rooted in the gospels and historic Christian faith.
It is not him. It is not a pastor, counselor, doctor, or prophet. It cannot replace real prayer, real scripture, or real people. It can, sometimes, help you find your way toward those things.
